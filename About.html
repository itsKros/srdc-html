Our story- Keeping Sydney smiling for over 20 years

Dr Thomas Vo established our Manly practice in 2001 with one vision in mind – to be the trusted practice that you think of for general, implant and cosmetic dentistry serving the northern beaches and Sydney.

With individualised treatment plans you will find that everything we do is designed to benefit you, for the long term. Our team takes care of your complete dental health at every stage of life.
